German Shepherd

German Shepherds are a remarkable breed, embodying both intelligence and loyalty in a compact package. They're renowned for their bravery and make wonderful companions for those who appreciate a small but mighty canine friend.

Pet Details

- Name: Rocky

- Age: 5 years

- Coat Color: Sable

- Weight: Around 80 pounds

- Temperament: Confident, loyal, and alert

- Health: Up-to-date vaccinations, regular vet check-ups, no known health issues

- Training: Basic obedience trained, knows commands like sit, stay, and come

- Activity Level: Moderate to high energy, enjoys daily walks and playtime

- Diet: Fed a balanced diet of high-quality dog food suitable for large breeds

- Socialization: Well-socialized with people and other dogs, but may be cautious around strangers
